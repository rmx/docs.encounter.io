Spell

Spells are actions which a unit can perform. A spell is cast on a target. This can be a WorldObject or a position on the terrain. A spell also has a cast time. That is the time during which the unit must stand still to fully concentrate on the spell casting. Performing any other action, or even simply moving, will cancel the spellcast.

cast(WorldObject) :: undefined

This function is called when the spell cast successfully executed. The first argument is the WorldObject target on which the spell was cast.
